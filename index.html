Cinnamon Squares

LETS LEARN TO MAKE CINNAMON SQAURES

ingreidents

  • Peanut Butter
  • Rasberry Jelly
  • 2 slices of bread

    • Instructions

    1. Mix the dry ingredients. In a medium bowl mix the flour, 2 teaspoons of cinnamon, the baking powder, and baking soda until fully combined, set aside
    2. Melt the butter and brown sugar. In a large saucepan, melt the 2/3 cup of butter together with the brown sugar over medium heat, stirring occasionally until smooth. Remove the pan from the heat and let cool slightly.
    3. Mix in eggs and vanilla. Stir the eggs and vanilla into the brown sugar and butter mixture until combined.
    4. Combine the dry and wet ingredients. Pour the dry ingredients into the saucepan or the wet ingredients into the dry and stir the ingredients together until a smooth batter forms.
      6. Pour the batter into the pan. Spread the batter into a 9 by 9 baking pan covered in aluminum foil and coated in cooking spray. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
    6. Sprinkle on the topping. In a small bowl mix the remaining 1 teaspoon of cinnamon, sugar, and 1 tablespoon of melted butter and sprinkle on top of the bars.
